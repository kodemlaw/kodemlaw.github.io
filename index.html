Know your Attorney!

Sharadha Kodem!

Sharadha Kodem is uniquely placed to help fellow immigrants. After completing her Bachelors in Computer Science from India, she arrived to United States on F1 to do her Masters in Computer Science and worked her way through the H1, EAD and eventually permanent residency. She has the personal experience with the heartache of missing the H1-B lottery and had the ordeal of dealing with deportation notice that was erroneously issued by USCIS while she was in law school. These experiences strengthened her resolve to help immigrants and upon graduation from Texas Wesleyan School of Law, she embarked on this mission.

She currently stays in Coppell with her husband and her two children. She enjoys learning new languages and is proficient in English, Hindi and Tamil and can converse in Telugu, Bengali and Gujarati reasonably well.
